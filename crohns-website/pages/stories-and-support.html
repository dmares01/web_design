Stories

There are plenty of places where you can find people who are willing to share their stories and their journeys with Crohn's disease. Check out places like instagram, the "Stolen Colon", the "Uncover Ostomy Blog", "Leaving the Seat Down". There are a lot of places to go for information


Support

Support means that you have the information you need when you need it. Your doctor will be your biggest source of support, but there are plenty of other resources as well. Check out the Crohns and Colitis website for more information.

Here is another webpage that has stories and a small blog as well. The best part is the Q and A section. Pretty much any question you have can be answered here if you are feeling brave enough to post it. Check it out here.
